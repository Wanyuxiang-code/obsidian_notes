注意 $\log(n!)$ 的量级  
证明：

$$
\begin{align}
& \text{On the one hand,}  \\
& \log(n!) = \sum_{k=1}^{n}\log(k) \leq n\log(n)  \\
& \text{On the other hand,} \\
& \log(n!) = \sum_{k=1}^{n}\log(k) \geq \log\left( \frac{n}{2}+1 \right) + \dots +\log(n) \geq \left( \frac{n}{2} \right)\log\left( \frac{n}{2} \right)
\end{align}
$$
